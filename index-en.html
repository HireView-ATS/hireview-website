Technology

Built to last,
Designed to perform.

HireView is built on a modern, robust, and scalable architecture designed to meet the demands of growing SMBs. The user interface is developed with React.js, ensuring a smooth and responsive experience across all devices. The backend relies on Node.js and Express, delivering high performance to handle large volumes of applications. Data is stored and secured in a PostgreSQL relational database, recognized for its reliability and robustness. User authentication is fully secured, ensuring protected login and fine-grained access control. Finally, hosting is powered by a high-availability cloud infrastructure, guaranteeing scalability suited to your team's growth.

Compliance & Security

HireView is fully
GDPR compliant.

Data protection is at the heart of HireView. Our platform is fully designed to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), in force across the European Union since May 2018.

🔒

EU-hosted data

All candidate data is stored on servers located within the European Union, fully compliant with GDPR requirements.

✅

Explicit consent

Candidates are informed about the use of their data and give explicit consent at every stage of the recruitment process.

🗑️

Right to erasure

Any candidate can request the deletion of their data at any time. HireView guarantees complete and permanent deletion within 72 hours.

📁

Access & portability

Candidates can access, edit, or export their data at any time, in full accordance with their GDPR rights.

🛡️

Data encryption

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest, ensuring maximum protection against unauthorized access.

📋

Processing register

HireView provides a record of processing activities to help you meet your obligations as a data controller.
